RFP Checklist for Evaluating Low-Code Platforms

Comprehensive checklist for evaluating low-code platforms based on internal methods (Gartner/KPMG/Accenture/DronaHQ/Pillir) and analysis of NextGen 11.7 with TCO/ROI and implementation examples.

1. Vendor Overview

✅ Full name, country of registration, legal form — NextGen, international development, corporate structure

✅ Year of foundation, team size, market experience — 10+ years, large team, experience in various industries

✅ Key clients, regions, industries — Banks, finance, retail, public sector, manufacturing, logistics, Digital Transformation, wide range of industries

2. Platform Architecture

✅ Cloud, on-premise, hybrid support — All deployment options, including Cloud

✅ Microservice/modular architecture — Modular, microservices supported

✅ Scalability, fault tolerance, disaster recovery — Enterprise load, DevOps, clustering

✅ Security certification (ISO 27001, SOC2, etc.) — Supported. Depends on implementation specifics and region

✅ API, SDK, marketplace support — REST API, SDK, plugins, no public marketplace

3. Core Capabilities

✅ Visual editor (drag-and-drop, workflow) — FormsDesigner, visual editors

✅ BPM/process automation tools — Agents, Maven, workflow engine

🔶 Web and mobile app development — Web, adaptive interface, no separate mobile SDK

✅ Versioning, rollback, publishing — Versioning, migrations, publishing supported

✅ Multichannel: email, SMS, messengers, chatbots — NotificationService, integration with external channels

4. Integration Layer

✅ REST, SOAP, GraphQL, Webhooks, TCP/IP support — REST, SOAP, webhooks, queues, no GraphQL out of the box

✅ Connectors to ERP, CRM, banking systems (SAP, Oracle, Microsoft, Salesforce, 1C, etc.) — IntegrationService, plugins, custom connectors

✅ SDK/API for custom integrations — SDK, REST API, extensibility

✅ ETL, BI/analytics, data pipelines — OLAP, BI integration, data export

✅ Legacy systems support — Plugins, custom connectors

5. Data Layer & DB Support

✅ DBMS: MSSQL, PostgreSQL, Oracle, MySQL, cloud DBs — All major DBMS supported

✅ Data governance, access control, audit trail — Audit, centralized audit, RBAC

✅ Import/export, data migration, data lineage — Import/export, migrations, lineage via audit

✅ Data warehouse/data lake, BI/analytics integration — BI integration, data export

✅ Storage scalability — Architecture supports scaling

6. Security & Compliance

✅ Authentication: SSO, LDAP, OAuth2, SAML — SSO, OAuth2, LDAP, corporate standards

✅ RBAC/ABAC, MFA, password policy — RBAC, MFA, password policy

✅ Encryption (at rest, in transit) — Encryption supported

✅ GDPR, ISO, regional law compliance — Supported. Depends on implementation specifics and region

✅ Logging, audit trail, incident response — Centralized audit, logging

7. Scalability & Performance

✅ Enterprise load, clustering — Clustering, scalability supported

✅ SLA, fault tolerance — SLA, monitoring, ApplicationTimeService

✅ Auto-scaling, DevOps/CI-CD — DevOps, CI/CD, containerization

✅ Metrics, monitoring, alerts — Monitoring, alerts, reports

8. UX/UI & User Experience

🔶 No-code/low-code tools for business — Visual editors, not full no-code

✅ Interface customization (themes, white label) — Customization, white label, themes

✅ Developer documentation (API, SDK) — Documentation, API, SDK

✅ Localization, multilingual — Multilingual support, localization (Arabic/English)

🔶 Online training, knowledge base, community — Knowledge base, training materials, no large open community

9. Ecosystem & Marketplace

🔶 Marketplace of ready modules/connectors — Plugins, extensibility, no public marketplace

✅ Third-party developer support, module publishing — SDK, API, extensibility

🔶 Integrator/VAR network, training, certification — Partner network, training, no mass certification

10. Support & Training

🔶 SLA (24/7, premium support) — Possible organizationally, technically supported

✅ Knowledge base, documentation, forum, chats — Knowledge base, documentation, chats

🔶 Training courses, user/partner certification — Courses and training, certification on request

✅ Support flexibility for industry/client — Flexible support, customization, localization

11. Pricing & Licensing

✅ Licensing: per user, per app, per usage, perpetual/SaaS — Flexible models, customer-friendly

🔶 Free trial/freemium — By agreement, depends on partner

✅ TCO transparency for 3-5 years — 5-year TCO: 60-75% savings (vs. traditional dev)

✅ Special rates (gov/large clients/startups) — Individual rates, government program support

✅ Upgrade/expansion terms — Flexible upgrade terms

12. Innovation & Roadmap

✅ AI/ML support, hyperautomation, advanced analytics — ScoringScripts, ML/AI, analytics

🔶 Public/private roadmap — Roadmap on request, not public

✅ Co-development with customer/partners — Possible, supported

13. Customer Success & References

✅ Implementation examples (scale, industry, region) — Public services, business, startups, wide range of industries

✅ Client feedback, NPS, user stories — Real scenarios from various industries

✅ Time-to-market, business effect (KPI, ROI) — MVP in 2-4 months, ROI 250-400%, 60-75% budget savings
