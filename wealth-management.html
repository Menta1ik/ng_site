Wealth Management

Build comprehensive wealth management solutions with a low‑code platform covering investment management, client portfolios, and digital services for financial advisors. Wealth Management enables personalized investment strategies, automated compliance, and enhanced client engagement.

Modeling Investment Processes

A visual editor allows you to design capital management strategies:

Client Assessment → Investment Profile Creation → Strategy Selection → Portfolio Monitoring → Reporting and Rebalancing.

Each process can be modified without rewriting the entire system, and business analysts and developers work in a single workspace.

Client Onboarding

  • Online questionnaires to define objectives and risk tolerance
  • Automatic KYC/AML checks
  • Creating a client profile in a unified system

The system immediately forms a client dossier and prepares recommendations.

Investment Scoring and Profiling

  • Determining the client's risk appetite
  • Selecting an investment strategy (conservative, balanced, aggressive)
  • Ability to incorporate ESG factors

This allows offers to be tailored to individual goals.

Investment Portfolio Management

  • Integration with brokerage and banking systems via API
  • Automatic asset allocation according to the selected strategy
  • Real-time performance monitoring

The platform supports a hybrid approach: automated management + advisor manual adjustments.

Financial Advisory

  • Built-in tools for advisors: reports, reminders, meeting playbooks
  • Support for hybrid models: robo-advisory + personal consultations
  • Chatbots and mobile apps for clients

This increases engagement and the quality of client service.

Reporting and Compliance

  • Generation of investment reports
  • Control of compliance with regulatory standards (MiFID II, Basel III, etc.)
  • Full audit trail of user actions

Clients and regulators get transparency and reliability.

Monitoring and Analytics

BI dashboards display:

  • Asset allocation by clients and strategies
  • Portfolio performance over time
  • Risks and deviations from target parameters
  • Advisor effectiveness

This helps a bank or investment company make strategic decisions.
