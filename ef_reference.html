Success Story

Full Online Lending Automation for eny Finance (Switzerland)

Client Overview

eny Finance AG

eny Finance AG is Switzerland's largest non-bank online lender, headquartered in Zurich. Since 2012, eny Finance has been offering fast, flexible personal loans entirely online, setting the benchmark for digital lending in Switzerland.

Challenge

To drive business growth and boost operational efficiency, eny Finance required a next-generation system for loan origination, servicing, scoring, collections, and reporting—all seamlessly integrated.

Key business needs included:

  • Automated borrower evaluation and scoring
  • Flexible, rule-based management of credit policies and business processes
  • Integration with multiple third-party services (credit bureaus, DMS, accounting, insurance, web front-end)
  • End-to-end reporting and compliance

Solution

After a thorough market evaluation, eny Finance selected the company and out-of-the-box lowcode platform renowned for its configurability, ease of use, and powerful automation capabilities.

The implementation included:

  • Migration from a legacy, in-house system to a unified new platform
  • Analysis and mapping of hundreds of internal documents and fields
  • Integration with two major credit bureaus (ZEK, CRIF), document management, accounting, insurance, and the public website
  • Automated onboarding, loan origination, scoring, servicing, and debt collection—all on a single platform
  • Support for dynamic business rules, AML filters, and compliance reporting
  • Transfer of the live lending business and historical loan portfolio into the new system

Implementation & Results

  • Seamless migration of data and business processes with minimal disruption to ongoing operations
  • Complex integrations with third-party providers, efficiently orchestrated by Company team
  • Agile adaptation to new business requirements, rapid changes and updates supported 24/7
  • Full automation of loan lifecycle, including reporting and compliance
  • Business growth enabled by faster product launches and scalable infrastructure

Client Feedback
