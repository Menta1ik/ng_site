About NextGen

NextGen is a low-code platform with over 10 years of continuous development, built on a foundation of deep expertise in banking, enterprise automation, and digital transformation.

Our journey began as an internal tool designed to deliver secure and robust solutions for mission-critical projects in the financial sector. This heritage ensured that NextGen was engineered from day one to meet the most demanding standards for security, compliance, and scalability.

With the onset of the global digital shift—accelerated by the COVID-19 pandemic—NextGen evolved from a proprietary tool to a standalone, market-ready low-code platform. Today, we empower organizations of all sizes to rapidly build, launch, and scale digital products, automate complex processes, and achieve true business agility.

Key advantages of NextGen:

  • Proven reliability in highly regulated industries
  • Modular, extensible architecture for any use case
  • Advanced integration and data management capabilities (ETL, BI, multi-DB)
  • High-performance and security by default
  • Fast onboarding and flexible deployment (cloud, on-premise, hybrid)
  • 24/7 expert support and continuous innovation

Our mission:

To make enterprise-grade digital automation accessible to every business, everywhere, without compromise on quality, security, or control.

Who we serve:

Banks, financial institutions, telecoms, public sector, retail, logistics, insurance, and ambitious digital-first enterprises.

Looking ahead:

We are open for strategic partnerships, co-innovation, and international expansion. Join us on the journey to the next generation of digital transformation.
