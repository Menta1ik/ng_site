Platform Story

The NextGen platform has a history of over 10 years of development. Originally, it was conceived and built as an internal tool, primarily focused on delivering robust and secure solutions for banking and enterprise clients. This focus enabled the platform to achieve a high level of maturity, stability, and compliance with the demanding standards of the financial sector.

Following the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic, it became evident that the global landscape for digital technologies and business continuity was fundamentally shifting. In response to these changes, a strategic decision was made to reorient NextGen from an internal tool towards a standalone product, ready for broader market adoption.

As a result, the absence of a visible market presence until recently is not indicative of a lack of product maturity or readiness, but rather a reflection of its origin as a proprietary solution developed for specific, mission-critical use cases. The current phase marks the platform's transition into an open, commercially available low-code offering, backed by years of proven performance in highly regulated industries.
