About NextGen

NextGen traces its origins back well before the term Low-Code Development Platform (LCDP) emerged in 2014, with its first projects dating as far back as 2010. Initially, the platform was positioned as an internal development environment — a set of powerful tools for building solutions in the banking sector.

From its early years, NextGen became the technological core for a range of specialized solutions: Anti-Fraud systems, scoring platforms, loan management systems (LMS), credit bureaus, analytics and reporting modules, BPMS, and audit systems.

As the client base expanded, the platform continued to evolve: a powerful process management engine was introduced, support for all major databases (MSSQL, Oracle, DB2, PostgreSQL, MySQL) was implemented, direct connections to external and internal data sources were enabled, and an integration service for two-way data exchange was developed. The platform transitioned to a modular architecture, enabling rapid deployment of custom-developed projects while ensuring scalability and high performance, including both synchronous and asynchronous modes of operation.

NextGen is fully compliant with international information security standards ISO/IEC 27001:2013, ISO/IEC 27002:2022, ISO/IEC 27017:2015, and ISO/IEC 27018:2019, as well as industry best practices from NIST and OWASP. Key technological measures include: multi-layer encryption (DPAPI + SSL/TLS + XML Encryption), client-specific data isolation, memory protection (.NET SecureString), digital signatures, flexible two-factor authentication, Active Directory integration, protection against SQL Injection and XSS attacks, as well as advanced access control and logging mechanisms.

While paying close attention to global and industry trends, NextGen has retained its distinctive development style and architectural approach, delivering comprehensive security, high performance, and adaptability for complex and highly regulated scenarios.

Key advantages of NextGen:

  • Proven reliability in highly regulated industries
  • Modular, extensible architecture for any use case
  • Advanced integration and data management capabilities (ETL, BI, multi-DB)
  • High-performance and security by default
  • Fast onboarding and flexible deployment (cloud, on-premise, hybrid)
  • 24/7 expert support and continuous innovation
