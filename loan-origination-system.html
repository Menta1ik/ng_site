Loan Origination System

From application intake to disbursement — NextGen low-code platform streamlines the entire loan origination lifecycle with automation, compliance, and analytics.

1 Application Intake & Pre-Processing

Collect, validate, and normalize applications across channels:

  • Multi-channel intake: web, mobile, partners
  • Automated data validation and enrichment
  • Initial KYC/AML screening with configurable rules

Enhanced capabilities: Dynamic forms minimize friction with progressive disclosure, while integrated data services pre-fill customer information to reduce errors.

2 Document Collection & Verification

Automate document intake and verification:

  • Capture identity, income, collateral, and financial documents
  • OCR and data extraction with validation rules
  • Biometric and liveness checks where required

Enhanced capabilities: Document pipelines include quality checks, duplicate detection, and automated follow-ups for missing items.

3 Risk Scoring & Underwriting

Flexible decisioning with rules, scorecards, and ML models:

  • Credit scoring with PD/LGD estimation
  • Policy rules and debt-to-income checks
  • Automated and manual underwriting workflows

Enhanced capabilities: Champion–challenger testing and cut-off calibration optimize approval strategies while maintaining risk appetite.

4 Offer Generation & E-Signature

Create compliant offers and finalize with digital signatures:

  • Personalized terms, pricing, and repayment schedules
  • Automated document generation
  • E-signature with MFA and audit trails

Enhanced capabilities: Real-time pricing engines and counter-offer workflows maximize conversion while meeting regulatory requirements.

5 Disbursement & Core Integration

Seamless integration with banking systems and third parties:

  • Core banking, ERP, and payment rails integration
  • Collateral registration and insurance partners
  • Event-driven updates and reconciliation

Enhanced capabilities: Retry patterns, idempotent APIs, and circuit breakers ensure reliable operations across external dependencies.

6 Monitoring & Analytics

Operational dashboards and performance reporting:

  • Time-to-yes and time-to-cash metrics
  • Approval rates, declines, and reasons taxonomy
  • Portfolio quality and early delinquency signals

Enhanced capabilities: Real-time alerts for SLA breaches and anomaly detection reduce operational risk and improve customer satisfaction.
